Privacy Policy for RIVLD

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to RIVLD ("RIVLD," "we," "our," or "us").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices you have when using the RIVLD mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By creating an account or using RIVLD, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Date of birth or age
  • Gender (optional, if provided)
  • Height
  • Weight

This information helps personalize your experience, improve scoring accuracy, and support fair competition within RIVLD.

B. Workout & Fitness Information

When using RIVLD, we collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Exercises performed
  • Sets, reps, and weight
  • Workout history
  • Personal records
  • Workout statistics
  • Achievements
  • Streaks
  • Session scores
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Gym ownership history
  • Competition history

These data are used to calculate scores, rankings, achievements, and other competitive features.

RIVLD is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.

C. Location Information

With your permission, RIVLD may access your device's location to:

  • Display nearby gyms
  • Help you discover gyms
  • Verify or associate workouts with a gym
  • Power map-based competition features
  • Improve location accuracy for rankings

Location information may include:

  • Precise GPS location (only with your permission)
  • Approximate location
  • Device-derived location information

RIVLD does not continuously track your location in the background unless explicitly disclosed and enabled in a future feature.

You may disable location access at any time through your device settings. Some location-based features may become unavailable.

D. Device & Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type
  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Language settings
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostic information
  • Usage analytics

This information helps us improve reliability, security, and performance.

E. Competition & Leaderboard Information

To operate RIVLD's competitive features, we may collect and display:

  • Username
  • Profile picture (if provided)
  • Gym affiliation
  • Rankings
  • Points
  • Leaderboard position
  • Achievements
  • Gym ownership status
  • Competition history

This information may be visible to other users as part of leaderboards, gym rankings, and other competitive experiences.

F. Push Notification Information

If you enable notifications, RIVLD may send notifications related to:

  • Friend requests
  • Friend request acceptance
  • Gym ownership
  • Crown changes
  • Rank changes
  • Workout reminders
  • Achievements
  • Streaks
  • Competition updates
  • Product announcements

Notification preferences may be managed through your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide the Services
  • Calculate scores, rankings, and achievements
  • Operate leaderboards and gym ownership
  • Display nearby gyms
  • Improve personalization
  • Recommend relevant gyms and competitors
  • Send notifications
  • Detect fraud, cheating, or abuse
  • Analyze performance and usage
  • Improve app functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the safety and security of our users

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

Service Providers

Trusted providers who help us operate RIVLD, including services for:

  • Cloud hosting
  • Authentication
  • Data storage
  • Analytics
  • Mapping services
  • Push notifications
  • Customer support

Other Users

Certain information is visible to other users, including:

  • Username
  • Profile picture (if provided)
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Points
  • Gym ownership
  • Competition achievements
  • Public profile information

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to:

  • Comply with applicable law
  • Respond to legal requests
  • Protect users
  • Investigate fraud or abuse
  • Protect our legal rights

Business Transfers

If RIVLD is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Third-Party Services

RIVLD may rely on trusted third-party providers to operate the Services.

These providers may include services for:

  • Authentication
  • Database hosting
  • Cloud infrastructure
  • Mapping
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • Push notifications

These providers process information only as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information.

While we strive to protect your information, no system or method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide the Services
  • Maintain your account
  • Operate leaderboards
  • Resolve disputes
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our agreements

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information unless we are legally required to retain certain data.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and personal information
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Object to certain processing
  • Request data portability where legally required

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

9. Children's Privacy

RIVLD is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age required in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

10. International Users

Your information may be stored or processed in countries other than your own where our service providers operate.

By using RIVLD, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred and processed outside your jurisdiction, subject to applicable law.

Any AI-generated content is provided for informational purposes only and should not be considered medical, fitness, or professional advice.
