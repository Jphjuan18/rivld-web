About RIVLD

RIVLD turns every gym into a competitive arena. Track your workouts, earn points, rise on leaderboards, and claim ownership of your gym.

  • Log exercises, sets, reps, and weight
  • Compete on gym and friend leaderboards
  • Battle for gym ownership and defend your crown
  • Discover nearby gyms with map-based competition
  • Connect with friends and rivals
  • Earn achievements, streaks, and personal records

RIVLD is built for fitness tracking and entertainment. It does not provide medical advice or professional fitness instruction.
